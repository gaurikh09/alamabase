ShieldGuard Inc. - Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Plan

Backup Procedures:
ShieldGuard implements automated daily incremental backups and weekly full backups of all customer data. Backups are encrypted using AES-256 and stored in geographically distributed data centers across three different AWS regions (US-East, US-West, and EU-Central).

Database backups are performed using continuous replication with a Recovery Point Objective (RPO) of 15 minutes. File storage backups maintain point-in-time snapshots for the past 30 days. All backup integrity is verified through automated restoration tests performed weekly.

Disaster Recovery:
Our disaster recovery plan is designed to achieve a Recovery Time Objective (RTO) of 4 hours for critical systems and 24 hours for non-critical systems. We maintain hot standby infrastructure in secondary regions that can be activated automatically in case of primary region failure.

Disaster recovery drills are conducted quarterly to validate our procedures and train staff. The most recent DR test in September 2023 achieved full system recovery in 2.5 hours, exceeding our RTO target.

Business Continuity Plans:
In the event of a major outage or disaster, ShieldGuard follows a documented business continuity plan that includes:

1. Immediate activation of the incident command center
2. Communication protocols for customers, employees, and stakeholders
3. Failover to backup data centers within 30 minutes
4. Alternative work arrangements for employees (remote work capabilities)
5. Vendor and supplier contingency plans

Our business continuity plan is reviewed and updated annually. Key personnel are trained on their roles and responsibilities during business continuity events. We maintain relationships with backup vendors for critical services.

Infrastructure Redundancy:
All critical infrastructure components are deployed with N+1 redundancy. Load balancers distribute traffic across multiple availability zones. Database systems use multi-master replication for high availability. Network connectivity includes redundant ISP connections with automatic failover.

Our platform maintains 99.95% uptime SLA, backed by service credits for any downtime exceeding this threshold. Historical uptime over the past 12 months has been 99.98%.
